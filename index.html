COVID - 19 Distress Relief Mission & Corona Care Bengaluru

The Good Quest Foundation since the outbreak of Covid-19 has continually engaged in relief work which attends to the health and economic distress caused by the deadly pandemic to the people. Since one year under the banner Good Quest Cares - Covid 19 Distress Relief Mission many schemes are launched and meticulously implemented helping the vulnerable communities recover from their state of helplessness caused by covid and lockdowns. The beneficiaries of our covid welfare schemes were daily wage labourers, migrants, poor, transgenders, refugees and tribals. Feeding the destitutes and slum dwellers, providing dry ration kit, providing nutritious food to the children from the vulnerable communities, providing aid to children to continue their education while at home during school lockdown, providing healthcare support and animal care are a few among the other notable schemes launched by us.

In the wake of the second wave, remaining sensitive to the needs of the people The Good Quest Foundation has joined hands with 4 other like minded ngos. The joint forum will be called 'CORONA CARE BENGALURU'. Food to your doorstep an initiative to provide quality, hygienic and food prepared under home environment to covid positive and home quarantined families is a flagship programme launched in the 2nd season of our Covid -19 relief work. Project Prana Vayu focuses on providing oxygen concentrators free of cost to the use of patients at home.

Project Prana Vayu

Project Prana Vayu is a timely response to the alarming need for Oxygen Support to people in Bangalore. Our service aims to help people gain better spo2 level until they could be assisted with hospital support. While Bangalore is one among such cities which continues to record highest Covid cases in a single day in India, Project prana vayu comes as a comfort to patients gasping for breathe. Our trained team of volunteers administer help to anyone who approaches us in need for oxygen support.

Project Prana Vayu is a joint initiayive by the Good Quest Cares - Covid-19 Distress Relief Mission and Corona Care Bengaluru supported by Karmodaya and DCMI.
